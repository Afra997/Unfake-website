How to Post & Vote Responsibly

Posting Guidelines

  • Please search with keywords before posting to avoid duplicate posts.
  • Share only information you believe to be true and can be verified.
  • Include credible sources or evidence when making a claim.
  • Avoid spreading rumors, unverified news, or sensational content.
  • Respect others—no hate speech, personal attacks, or offensive language.
  • Report any suspicious or harmful posts to the admins.

Voting Guidelines

  • Vote "True" only if you are confident the information is accurate and supported by facts.
  • Vote "False" if you have evidence or credible sources disproving the claim.
  • Do not vote based on personal bias or popularity—focus on facts.
  • Read the post and any linked sources carefully before voting.
  • If unsure, refrain from voting or seek more information.

Why Responsible Participation Matters

Your actions help keep the community safe and informed. Responsible posting and voting prevent the spread of misinformation and build trust among users. Thank you for contributing thoughtfully!

Warning: Consequences of Fake or Harmful Behavior

  • Accounts found spreading fake news, misinformation, or harmful content may be banned without warning.
  • Repeated violations can result in permanent suspension of your account.
  • Severe cases may be reported to relevant authorities as per community guidelines and local laws.
  • All actions are logged and monitored to protect the integrity of the platform.
